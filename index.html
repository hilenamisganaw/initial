Hilena Misganaw

Rising Lower, Social Justice Activist, Recently-Realized Renaissance Woman

Here's a brief synopsis containing basic information about me:

My name is Hilena Misganaw and I'm from the nawf- Atlanta, Georgia to be specific. I'm a rising lower at Phillips Academy and will be living in Johnson House. I have two brothers, one is 16 and the other is 9, and live with them as well as with my mother and father. I come from a religious community of Ethiopian-Americans, and as a result, am very close with my faith and the people I share it with: the other kids at church. I love music and singing is a big hobby of mine. My friends and I and Andover bond over music, just a testament to it's power. I've always wanted a career in medicine, surgery specifically, but Andover has revealed to me my passion for social justice issues (and maybe computer science one day).
